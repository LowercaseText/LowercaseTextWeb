You and your stickman friends are trying to collect the biggest diamond collection ever. Sadly you don't have the money to buy them. Thankfully you can always steal them, and that's your job. Just be sneaky about it... Unless you woke up and chose violence today.

this game will feature:

  1. A lot of stickmen
  2. some cars
  3. big guns (american)
  4. valueble things to steal
  5. a lot of levels
  6. a story
  7. some secrets
  8. a lot of traps
  9. some guards
  10. a crap ton of puzzles
  11. a level editor
  12. a character customizer

Development info

Progress on this game is very slow. I keep getting distracted by doing other projects, as well as the code for this game is getting very messy, However I still aim to finish this game. I like this game and the Idea a lot. I will probably make devlogs/updates on this game in my blog.
